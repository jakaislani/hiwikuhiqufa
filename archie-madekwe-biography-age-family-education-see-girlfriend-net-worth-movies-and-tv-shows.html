See is an upcoming American drama web television series produced for Apple TV+. The author is Steven Knight and directed by Francis Lawrence. Executive producers include Knight, Lawrence, Peter Chernin, Jenno Topping, and Kristen Campo. Madekwe portrayed Kofun in the series.
