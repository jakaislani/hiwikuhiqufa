Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, April 27, 2024

Netizens wish to know about John Barnett of Boeing Wikipedia as the man revealed that the company was not up to the mark.

John Barnett provided his services at the aerospace company for nearly three decades. For seven years, he worked in North Charleston, South Carolina.

Later, in 2010, he was transferred to Everett, Washington to work as a quality manager. When he noticed some defects and complained to his bosses, they ignored him and moved him to another production plant.

Who Is John Barnett Of Boeing? Wikipedia Explored

John Barnett, who worked 30 years at Boeing is yet to be awarded a Wikipedia profile.

Despite spending several years at the aerospace company, he revealed that there were severe problems in its production. During his time there, he noticed metal slivers on wirings that controlled flight commands.

Indeed, this is a serious issue and he reported to his bosses. To his surprise, they ignored him and asked him to work at another part of the production plant.

Moreover, he claims that the flights out of Charleston cannot be called safe and airworthy. As a quality manager, he gave his best but was never satisfied with the company’s production.

Along with John, other several workers confirmed that the upper management used to ignore their problems as well. According to him, the employees were under pressure and were mostly working to get their job done for the day.

Reportedly, he believes that none of them were thinking about the long-term ramifications. Also, he alleged that Boeing was focused on speed and cost-cutting rather than focusing on public safety.

Recently, he was spotted in Netflix‘s Downfall: The Case Against Boeing. The show focuses on the Boeing controversy two horrific aircraft crashed which resulted in the death of 350 people.

Where Is John Barnett Of Boeing Now?

John Barnett of Boeing is now retired and his last known location is in South Carolina.

According to The Cinemaholic, he retired from the company in 2017. Two years later, he filed a lawsuit against Boeing claiming that they had denigrated his character.

Moreover, he confirmed that his career was greatly hampered after he addresses the issues of the production. This was the major reason why John had to retire early.

Furthermore, he revealed that he was harassed at his workplace. Also, he dealt with severe anxiety attacks.
