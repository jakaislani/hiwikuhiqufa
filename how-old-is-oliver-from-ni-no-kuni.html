How old is Oliver from Ni no Kuni?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 22, 2024
The game's main playable character, Oliver, is a 13-year-old boy. The team decided to make him a child as they wished to showcase a coming of age story. They wanted children to empathize with Oliver's development, and adults to relive the excitement of their adolescence.
