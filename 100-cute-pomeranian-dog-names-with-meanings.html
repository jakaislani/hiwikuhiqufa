100+ Cute Pomeranian Dog Names With Meanings

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, April 28, 2024

Picking a dog’s name is exciting as well as an intimidating job. Pomeranian’s are small canines with friendly and outgoing in nature. You want a name that resembles their jolly nature and your love for them perfectly. Pomeranian’s deserve the finest names despite their color and size.

With some stylish Pomeranian haircuts, they can look even more adorable like foxes or teddy bears. We have enlisted a bunch of names that will suit well for your new Pom Pom puppy.

Popular Names for Pomeranian Dogs

If you don’t want to go wrong with your Pomeranian’s name, give them one of these popular names.

1. Winston

Winston is a British name with the meaning “joyful stone.”

2. Rocky

The meaning of this English name, Rocky, is “rest.”

3. Prince

Prince is a male Pomeranian name of Latin origin, meaning “royal son.”

4. Izzy

Izzy is a cool name of Hebrew origin and means “God’s promise.”

5. Lucky

This name is of American origin and means “fortunate.”

6. Bella

Bella means “beautiful” in both Latin and Italian origin.

7. Rosie

Rosie is a beautiful name for a female pomeranian. In Latin, this name means “rose.”

8. Charlie

Charlie is a name of German origin that means “free man.”

9. Scout

Scout is a name that stems from Old French origins and means “sent to listen” or “to discover.”

10. Stella

Stella is a cute name for a female Pomeranian that refers to “star.”

11. Bruno

Bruno is a cute name that originates from the Old German “Brun,” meaning “brown.”

12. Luna

The meaning of the name Luna is “moon.”
