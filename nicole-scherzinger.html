Nicole Scherzinger- Biography

Nicole Scherzinger is a well-known American pop singer and reality TV star. Scherzinger rose to prominence after appearing in the reality television show Popstars. She is also best known as the main singer of the ‘Pussycat Dolls.’

Nicole Scherzinger- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Education

Nicole Scherzinger was born on June 29, 1978, in Honolulu, Hawaii, in the United States. Her full name is Nicole Prescovia Elikolani Valiente. Her astrological sign is cancer, and she is 43 years old. She is of Filipino descent. Her parents are her father, Alfonso Valiente, and her mother, Rosemary Elikolani. Nicole’s parents divorced when she was a child. Her mother then married Gary Scherzinger, and she adopted his surname. She also has a sister named Keala Scherzinger in her family. Scherzinger is a devout Christian.

In terms of education, she started at the Youth Performing Arts School. She continued her education at DuPont Manual High School. After graduating from high school, she enrolled at Wright State University in Dayton, Ohio. She also majored in Theatre and Dance. Scherzinger dropped out of college after receiving an offer from the popular band ‘Days of the New.’

Nicole Scherzinger- Relationship, Married Life

Nicole Scherzinger is engaged to her boyfriend Thom Evans, and she is straight. Thom is a retired Scottish rugby player. They got engaged in the year 2020. From 2000 to 2004, Scherzinger was married to 311, lead vocalist Nick Hexum.

Singer Scherzinger began a high-profile romance after meeting at the 2007 MTV Europe Music Awards in Munich. It was with the defending Formula One World Champion, Lewis Hamilton. Their on-again, an off-again seven-year relationship was well-documented.

They divorced for the final time in February 2015. In 2016, she began a romantic engagement with Bulgarian tennis player Grigor Dimitrov, which she dissolved in early 2019. In January 2020, Scherzinger confirmed her engagement to former Scottish rugby player Thom Evans.

Nicole Scherzinger- Professional Career

Nicole Scherzinger began her career as a member of the rock band Days of the New. After winning the WB television show Popstars, she went on to form the all-girl band Eden’s Crush in 2001. She made her debut as the lead singer and frontwoman of the Pussycat Dolls in 2003. The band published their debut album in 2005, which included the singles Don’t Cha, Stickwitu, and Buttons. She and her band released Doll Domination in 2008, which included the singles I Hate This Part and When I Grow Up.

She quit the band in 2009 to pursue a solo career, and her debut solo single, “Jai Ho,” from the 2008 film Slumdog Millionaire, was a huge success, hitting number one in seventeen countries. “Killer Love,” her debut solo album, was released in 2011. Her second studio album, “Big Fat Lie,” was released in 2014. She has also appeared in films such as Moana and Men in Black 3.

She also served as a judge on The X Factor in the United Kingdom, alongside Simon Cowell and Paula Abdul. Nicole has also judged Australia’s Got Talent. She also starred as Penny in ABC’s Dirty Dancing, which premiered on May 24, 2017. Scherzinger voiced a character in Ralph Breaks the Internet in 2018. Scherzinger made her debut as a panelist on the reality competition series The Masked Singer in January 2019.
