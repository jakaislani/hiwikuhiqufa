Welcome to Derry set pics suggest Pennywise could be deviously lurking around pickle food truc

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Ever since It prequel series Welcome to Derry was officially announced, passionate horror fanatics and devout Stephen King die-hards have had their eyes glued to the headlines lately in hopes of receiving a slew of breadcrumbs. But luckily, eagle-eyed fans won’t have to wait too long to score a first glimpse at the upcoming series — which is set to explore villain Pennywise’s twisted origin story. But before exclusive behind-the-scenes images are launched, fans are first being rewarded with a few intriguing set pics.

Recommended Videos

Over on the r/horror subreddit, user u/Dances-with-Scissors happily shared several images from the set of the much-anticipated horror series — which is currently filming in Toronto, Canada, right now. The three images showcase a handful of food vendors and markets that children back in the ‘50s typically hung around. You can check out the post for yourself down below:

Among the images, the first pic highlights a pickle food truck called Melvin’s. Considering the Losers Club is notorious for hanging around the streets of Derry during the events of It, it would certainly make sense that kids from the ‘50s were hanging around the same spots — which would only mean that Pennywise is lurking nearby just waiting to feast.

Of course, these images are merely minor details at this point, but it shouldn’t be long before showrunner Andy Muschietti shares an array of eye-popping images that will simply increase the series’ growing hype. We’ll have to wait and find out when Welcome to Derry arrives on HBO Max in the future.
