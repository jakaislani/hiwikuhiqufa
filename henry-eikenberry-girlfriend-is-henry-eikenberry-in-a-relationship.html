Henry Eikenberry girlfriend: Is Henry Eikenberry in a relationship?

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Henry Eikenberry has not revealed any information about his relationship status to the public, hence it is unknown whether the 24-year-old star is currently single or dating anyone.

However, Eikenberry who starred in Season 2 of the HBO series, previously dated a woman named Coco Hill but it’s unclear when the pair called it quits.

Henry Eikenberry is an American actor.

He graduated from Brooklyn Friends School in Downtown Brooklyn, New York City in June 2017.

Henry made his professional acting debut in 2022.

He shot to fame when he starred in Euphoria season 2 (2022), playing Derek, the focal character in a revelatory love story following Jacob Elordi’s father Cal in his adolescent years.

WME signed Eikenberry for representation in May 2022. He subsequently got a key role in Apple TV+’s The Crowded Room (2023), a seasonal anthology series from Akiva Goldsman and New Regency.
