Vamshi Krishna is a film actor, hailing from India, and worked in the Telugu and Tamil cinema. The tall and hunky man was born on 31st March 1986 in Visakhapatnam. He belongs to a family of doctors but he didn’t want to be a doctor. He wanted to start his own business so he went to London for a Management course. During studies, he met a director, Gautham Menon who offered him a supporting role in his movie “Gharshana”. The Indian actor accepted the role and made a debut with a movie in which he portrayed a supporting role of a police officer. The film was released in 2004.

Career

Vamsi Krishna started his career after making a debut movie “Gharshana” which was a Gautham Menon’s directorial of 2004. Later, he appeared in a lead role as a villain in the movie “Thadaiyara Thaakka”, “Ivan Veramathiri” and “Naitaandi”. However, he frequently appeared in Tamil movies and became popular.

In 2006, Vamsi Krishna also appeared in a Telugu movie “Oka V Chitram” in which he played a lead role. In the film, he worked alongside Aadhi. Later, he played roles in various movies like; “Darling”, Attaintiki Daredi” and Nenu Naa Rakshasi” as well.

However, he has worked in various Tamil and Telugu movies and marked his identity in the cinematic world. Undoubtedly, he is a star who has been noticed by everyone as many people love and know him.

The actor has a charismatic and dashing personality which is enough to steal the hearts of many. And undoubtedly, we can say that he is the man who is blessed with amazing acting skills.

Achievements

Among various notable achievements, he earned a nomination of the Vijay Award for the category of Best Villain.
