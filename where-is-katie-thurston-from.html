Where is Katie Thurston from? The Bachelorette stars life and career explored! Tue 8 June 20

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, July 14, 2024

Katie Thurston’s season of The Bachelorette premiered on Monday, June 7.

The former Bachelor contestant has embarked upon her journey to find love and fans are thrilled to tag along.

The Bachelorette season 17’s premiere episode saw 34 contestants walk in to claim Katie’s heart and possibly her hand in marriage. However, many viewers feel that they are yet to know more about the Bachelorette herself.

Where is Katie Thurston from?

The season 17 Bachelorette, Katie’s bio on ABC reveals that she hails from Renton city, Washington.

From her Instagram, it looks she currently lives in Seattle. Right before starting her The Bachelor gig, Katie bought a new house. Taking to her Instagram stories the reality star said, it was the biggest accomplishment of her life.

View Instagram Post

From her social media platforms, it appears that the Bachelorette prefers to keep her family life under wraps. Not much is known about her parents or siblings.

However, in a conversation with Sarah Trott on Matt James’ The Bachelor season, Katie had revealed that her dad passed away in 2012. In the heartfelt conversation, she revealed, “I missed out on my goodbye to my dad. I would never want that for you.”

Katie also encouraged her fellow contestant to be with her terminally ill father.

Katie Thurston’s occupation revealed!

Katie is a Bank Marketing Manager in Washington. However, the reality star has revealed in her ABC bio, that she wishes to host her own talk show one day. 

It looks like Katie’s Bachelor gig might just land her in the right circles to be able to do achieve her dream.

The 30 year old has an impressive following on TikTok. She has over 300,000 followers and creates ample amount of content for the platform.

What is Katie Thurston’s ethnicity?

Katie, born on January 3, 1991, in Lynnwood, Washington is of Caucasian ancestry.

Katie Thurston’s journey on The Bachelor

On Matt’s The Bachelor season, Katie became one of leading voices on the show.

She was loved by fans for standing up against bullying and negativity in the house. She also stood up for her fellow contestant Sarah who was being bullied in the house.

Many took to Twitter at the time and applauded Katie’s action and personality.
