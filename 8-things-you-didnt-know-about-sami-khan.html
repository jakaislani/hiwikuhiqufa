8 Things You Didnt Know About Sami Khan

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, June 11, 2024

➡ Sami Khan Biography

Sami Khan is a Pakistani actor who works in Pakistani films and television serials for which he got immense love and much fame. He took the start of his professional career through a debut film titled ‘Salakhein’ and then moved to television. Since then, he has given continuously amazing performances that won the hearts of many. However, his fans still want to know more about him, so we have compiled some unknown things they probably did not know about.

8 Things You Didn’t Know About Sami Khan

1- Sami Khan is not his real name

For people, he has been Sami Khan, but he was born Mansoor Aslam Khan Niazi, he then made it shorter into Sami Khan to make it more suitable for showbiz.

2- Sonu Nigam sang the first song filmed on Sami Khan

In 2004, when Sami Khan did a debut film ‘Salakhain’, the Bollywood singer Sonu Nigam sang the first song filmed on him.

3- Achievements

Among various notable achievements, Sami Khan was honored with both the PTV Award and Tamgha-e-Imtiaz.

4- His elder brother resembles with him

Sami Khan has an elder brother Taifoor Khan who is also working in showbiz as an actor and director. Taifoor Khan resembles Sami Khan.

5- Appearance in the music video

Sami Khan was featured in the music video titled ‘Mahiya’ by Annie Khalid. The music video was directed by his brother Taifoor Khan.

6- Appearance in the Ramadan Transmission

Ramadan Transmission segment became the most popular segment of the year as he co-hosted twice. Once in Ittihad Ramadan for A Plus, aired in 2017 and second one when the transmission moved to Geo Entertainment in 2018.

7- Popular Serials

Sami Khan did many popular serials for which he got huge acclaim during his career life. Some of them include ‘Saraab’ starring Sonya Hussain and Nazish Jahangir, ‘Dulhan’, starring Farish Shafi and Sumbul Iqbal Khan, and others.

8- He & Social Media

The charming actor is an active user of social media as he loves interacting with his fans and followers particularly on his Instagram account.
