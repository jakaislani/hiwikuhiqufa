Early Life and Career

Mychal Thompson was born in Nassau, Bahamas, but moved to Miami, Florida, at a young age. His basketball career began in high school and continued at the University of Minnesota. He was the first overall pick in the 1978 NBA Draft, selected by the Portland Trail Blazers. Thompson’s NBA career spanned 13 seasons, during which he played for the Trail Blazers, San Antonio Spurs, and Los Angeles Lakers.

NBA Career and Earnings

Thompson’s NBA career was marked by consistent performance and significant contributions to his teams. His career earnings from the NBA are a substantial part of his net worth. He won two NBA championships with the Los Angeles Lakers in 1987 and 1988, further cementing his legacy in the sport.

Post-NBA Career
