Where Is Karen Demel Now?

Jerry Buss’ ex-girlfriend, Karen Demel, is the mother of two of his children. Buss was a businessman, investor, scientist, and philanthropist from the United States.

He was the main owner of the Los Angeles Lakers professional basketball club in the National Basketball Association (NBA). His club has won ten league titles, the most notable of which came during the team’s Showtime period in the 1980s.

As a contributor, he is a member of the Naismith Memorial Basketball Hall of Fame. Other professional sports franchises in Southern California were owned by Buss.

He was a self-made man and had a rags-to-riches story that epitomized the American Dream. Buss, a Great Depression-era youngster living in poverty in Wyoming, faced the challenges of a blue-collar existence and began working at an early age.

Buss began his career working for his stepfather’s plumbing company, rising up at 4:30 a.m. to dig trenches on freezing ground for three hours before school.

Later in high school, Buss worked for two bucks a day at a nearby motel.

He dropped out of school to work for the railroad, later returning to get his undergraduate degree from the University of Wyoming. He also got a Ph.D. in physical chemistry from the University of Southern California.

Saving money from his first job after graduating from USC, Buss gathered four other partners in 1959 to acquire a 14-unit rental property in West Los Angeles.

He made his first investment with a $6,000 down payment and a $100,000 bank loan.

Buss transformed this $6,000 down purchase into a $350 million real estate empire eighteen years later.
